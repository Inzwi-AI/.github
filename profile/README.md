# Inzwi

Voice AI for Shona — built by Shona speakers, for Shona speakers.

Shona has roughly 15 million speakers and effectively no production-quality
voice AI. Mainstream models can stumble through written translation; none
hear or speak it the way the language is actually used. Inzwi is the
community-built corpus, the platform that collects it, and (eventually)
the speech models that come out the other side.

## What we're building

- A platform where Shona speakers contribute the raw data Shona AI
  needs: voice recordings, free-form conversation, English &lt;-&gt; Shona
  translations, and peer-validated transcriptions.
- The first openly developed Shona speech dataset, with provenance,
  versioning, and a clear licence path.
- Speech models (ASR first, TTS later) trained on that dataset and
  released alongside it.

## How to help

- **Contribute** — read a sentence, answer a question, translate a line,
  validate a peer's work, all at <https://inzwi.app>. Works without an
  account; sign up to claim points + streaks.
- **Read in Shona on WhatsApp** — message the bot directly. Voice notes,
  text, your call.
- **Spread it** — Zimbabwean friends, family WhatsApp groups, diaspora
  alumni chats. Volume of speakers is the entire game.
